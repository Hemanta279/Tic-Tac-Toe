Open index.html in your web browser.
Player 'O' starts the game.
Click on any empty box to place your mark (either 'O' or 'X').
Players alternate turns until one player successfully places three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row.
The game will announce the winner or declare a draw.
